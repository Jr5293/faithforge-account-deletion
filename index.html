Delete Your Account In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the Faith Forge app:

Open Faith Forge App
Launch the app on your device
Go to Settings

Find "Danger Zone"
Scroll to the bottom of the settings page

Request Deletion via Email

If you're unable to use the in-app deletion feature, you can request account deletion by email:

Send your deletion request to:

support@faithforge.app

Please include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is permanent
üö® Important: Data Deletion Details

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User Profile: Name, email, preferences, and settings
  • Prayer Data: All prayer requests, logs, and history
  • Community Data: Friend connections, encouragements, and interactions
  • Progress Data: Goals, achievements, and tracking history
  • Saved Content: Bookmarks, highlights, and notes
  • Local Data: All app data stored on your device

This action cannot be undone. Your data cannot be recovered once deleted.

Data Retention Policy

Once your deletion request is processed:

  • Your account and personal data are immediately deleted from our systems
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 90 days for service improvement
  • Legal or regulatory requirements may require us to retain certain transaction records
  • Backup systems are purged within 30 days of deletion

Processing Timeline

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate processing
  • Email Requests: Processed within 3-5 business days
  • Complete Data Removal: Within 30 days from all systems and backups
